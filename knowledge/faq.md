Traditional portfolios are passive. I wanted to build an active demonstration of my engineering skills. Instead of just listing "FastAPI" or "DevOps" on a static PDF, this RAG-powered application proves my ability to design hybrid search (vector + BM25), implement caching strategies (Redis), set up containerized environments (Docker/NGINX), construct deployment pipelines, and engineer self-healing LLM flows.

---

## What is your preferred technology stack?
I enjoy working with a modern, performant, and type-safe stack:
- **Backend**: FastAPI (Python), Node.js (TypeScript)
- **Database**: PostgreSQL (with pgvector), Redis
- **DevOps**: Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform, ArgoCD, GitHub Actions
- **Frontend**: React, Tailwind CSS

I am language-agnostic and quick to adapt to new environments or proprietary tooling as project demands shift.

The easiest way is to use the `/hire` command right here in the terminal interface! Submit your contact details (email, company, and message), and I will receive an instant notification. Alternatively, you can email me directly or connect with me via LinkedIn.

## Are you open to contract or freelance opportunities?
While my primary goal is a permanent, full-time engineering role, I am open to short-term consulting or contract opportunities if the engineering challenges align with my expertise in DevOps migrations, RAG implementations, or API performance tuning.
